Top Activities To Do In Islamabad

Pakistan Monument

The Pakistan Monument is a national monument and heritage museum located on the western Shakarparian Hills in Islamabad, Pakistan.

Daman-E-Koh

Daman-e-Koh is a hilltop garden north of Islamabad. It is in the center of the Margalla Hills. It is 2400ft above sea level.

Faisal Mosque

The Faisal Mosque is the national mosque of Pakistan, located in Islamabad. It is the sixth-largest mosque in the world, the largest mosque outside the Middle East
